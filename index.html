Projects

Extracting Key Patterns in Text Data Using SVD

Academic project completed at AIMS Rwanda, Implemented in Julia.

  • Developed a text analysis project to extract hidden semantic patterns from a collection of documents.
  • Converted raw text into a structured numerical form for document comparison and semantic analysis.
  • Applied Singular Value Decomposition (SVD) to reduce dimensionality and uncover latent relationships in text data.
  • Used Latent Semantic Analysis (LSA) to study how documents are related beyond direct word matching.
  • Computed document similarity to evaluate how closely texts are connected in semantic space.
  • Implemented query projection to compare search terms with the existing document collection.
  • Generated visual outputs to support the interpretation of semantic similarity patterns.
  • Demonstrated how linear algebra and computational methods can be used in text mining and pattern extraction.

Ion-Acoustic Solitary Waves in Magnetized Plasma

Undergraduate research project completed at UR-CST.

  • Conducted a final year research project on ion-acoustic solitary waves in a magnetized plasma system.
  • Developed a theoretical plasma model involving Tsallis distributed electrons and cold ions.
  • Applied the Sagdeev potential method to study the existence and propagation of nonlinear solitary waves.
  • Derived the energy-like equation and Sagdeev potential governing wave behavior in the plasma system.
  • Investigated the effects of Mach number, temperature ratio, magnetic field obliqueness, and non-extensive parameter on solitary wave dynamics.
  • Performed symbolic and numerical computations in Python.
  • Produced graphical results in Python to analyze changes in wave amplitude, width, and stability.
  • Connected the findings to applications in space plasma physics and laboratory plasma modelling.
